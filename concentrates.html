Blue Cheese Flower Rosin

Hand-pressed Rosin Rosin is a form of marijuana concentrate that harvests the trichome heads off the plant matter by pressure and heat. Shatter, on the other hand, uses CO2or butane to separate the trichomes from the plant matter. Rosin is great for dabbing, the hit is cleaner and the high is euphoric and long-lasting. The fact that there are no chemicals used, makes the flavor profile very dynamic.  
Flavour Profile: Blue Berry, CheeseFlavour Profile: Earthy, Sweet
